Team of lawyers

The law firm is composed of lawyers that belong to different generations of professionals, who are in constant interaction and study to enhance their experience and skills.

Our professionals are supported by secretaries and assistants that have decades of working exclusively in law. Also, the law firm has a wide network of support in very different areas, for example legal experts in engineering, accounting, mechanics, graphology, documentaries, aeronautical, geology, health areas, as well as researchers and security.

Lawyers who have been trained in this law firm have become court ministers and have held other important public and private positions.
